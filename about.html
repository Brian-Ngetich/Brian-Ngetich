Our Story

Built for
the Ride

VeloRide began in 2017 in a small workshop with one obsession: building bikes that feel alive under you. We combined traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering to create something truly different.

Today, our bikes are ridden across five continents — from city streets to alpine trails. We believe every rider deserves a machine that matches their ambition.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Precision Engineering

Every component is selected with obsessive attention to quality, weight, and performance. We don't compromise.

Sustainability

Our production process uses renewable energy and recycled materials. Riding a bike is already green — we make it greener.

Rider-First

Every design decision starts with the rider. We test with real cyclists on real terrain before anything goes to production.
